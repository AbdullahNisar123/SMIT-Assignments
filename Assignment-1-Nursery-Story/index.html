The Brave Little Rabbit

Once upon a time, in a quiet forest, there was a little rabbit named Remy. Remy was very brave, even though he was the smallest rabbit in the forest.

One day, while Remy was hopping through the woods, he noticed something strange shiny under a tree. It was a M. Key. He knew he had to take it to the wise old owl, Professor Hoot.

The Adventure Begins

Remy decided to go on an adventure. The forest can be scary, he thought, but I can do this!

He hopped past tall trees and rustling bushes until he came across a big river.
He couldn't swim, but he found a strong branch to use as a bridge.

"Bravery isn't about being fearless," said Professor Hoot. "It's about doing what's right even when you're scared."

With the key in hand, Remy returned to the forest, feeling proud. The animals cheered for him, and from that day on, he was known as the 1st bravest rabbit in the forest!

Note: Always believe in yourself, no matter how small you are.

The End.
